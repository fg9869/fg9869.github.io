Common Excercises

The following are common excercises that we encourage our clients to do as part of their daily routine.

Burpee
Burpees are great, full body excercise to increase your strength and endurance.Begin in a standing position, and drop into a squat and extend your hands forward.
Plank
Planks built your core strength. To perform the plank, get in a pushup position.
Mountain Climber
Mountain climbers are a good cardio excercise. Start by placing your hands on the floor in a pushup position.
